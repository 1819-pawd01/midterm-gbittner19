Buffalo Chicken Dip!

This Buffalo Chicken Dip tastes like Buffalo Chicken wings without the mess. Serve hot with celery sticks or veggies!

What is Needed?

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of shreeded cooked chicken
  • 8 Ounces of softened cream cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Red Hot
  • 1/2 Cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 Cup blue cheese crumbles

How to Make?

Steps!

  1. Preheat Oven
    1. 350 degrees farenheit
  2. Combine all ingredients and spoon it into a shallow one quart baking dish
  3. Bake for 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through
  4. Stir
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired
  6. Serve with crackers or vegetables

The prep time for buffalo chicken dip is a short five minutes with a cooking time of 20 minutes. This recipe makes up to four ups of dip.
